FAQ about Mortgages

Learn how inflation affects your mortgage and how to manage your finances!



How does inflation affect variable-mortgage rates? Could increase or decrease your mortgage rate.

A variable mortgage rate is the prime rate (i.e: the interest rate that commercial banks charge clients) plus or minus the bank’s mortgage rate discount. Thus, an increase in inflation could potentially increase or decrease one’s mortgage rate.

 How does inflation affect fixed-mortgage rates? May indirectly increase fixed-mortgage rate.

An increase in inflation rate is not directly linked to an increase in a fixed-mortgage rate. However, a bond yield may increase as inflation increases which may, in turn, increase your fixed-mortgage rate. A bond-yield is essentially the return you get on a bond.

 Will mortgage rates go up in 2024? Expected to decrease.

Current studies and predictions estimate that mortgage rates will decrease by mid-2024 however this is subject to change.
